Passing Techniques

Mastering passing is crucial for team play and game control. Here are the key techniques and common mistakes to be aware of.

Key Techniques

  • Proper body positioning before the pass
  • Correct follow-through motion
  • Check surroundings before passing
  • Control the weight of the pass
  • Use the inside of the foot for accuracy

Common Mistakes

  • Poor body positioning
  • Incorrect follow-through
  • Not checking surroundings
  • Inconsistent weight of pass
  • Rushing the pass without proper technique

Body Positioning Tips

  • Plant non-kicking foot beside the ball
  • Keep your head down during contact
  • Maintain balance throughout the pass
  • Follow through in the direction of the pass
  • Keep your body over the ball

Safety Tips

  • Always warm up properly before training
  • Focus on proper technique before power
  • Build up distance gradually
  • Take rest days between intense sessions
  • Stay hydrated and maintain proper nutrition
