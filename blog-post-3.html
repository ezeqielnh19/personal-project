Dribbling Techniques

Mastering dribbling is essential for creating opportunities and maintaining possession. Here are the key techniques and common mistakes to be aware of.

Key Techniques

  • Keep your head up to scan the field
  • Use small, controlled touches
  • Change pace and direction effectively
  • Use both feet for better control
  • Maintain proper body positioning

Common Mistakes

  • Looking down at the ball
  • Taking too large touches
  • Predictable movement patterns
  • Not using both feet
  • Poor body positioning

Body Positioning Tips

  • Stay on your toes
  • Keep knees slightly bent
  • Maintain low center of gravity
  • Use arms for balance
  • Keep body between ball and defender

Safety Tips

  • Always warm up properly before training
  • Focus on control before speed
  • Build up intensity gradually
  • Take rest days between intense sessions
  • Stay hydrated and maintain proper nutrition
