Finesse Shot Techniques

Learn the art of the finesse shot - a powerful technique for scoring goals with precision and curve.

Key Techniques

  • Proper foot placement
  • Ball contact point
  • Follow-through motion
  • Body positioning
  • Curve generation

Common Mistakes

  • Incorrect foot placement
  • Poor ball contact
  • Insufficient follow-through
  • Wrong body angle
  • Over-curving the shot

Body Positioning Tips

  • Plant non-kicking foot beside ball
  • Keep body slightly open
  • Maintain balance throughout
  • Use arms for stability
  • Follow through in shooting direction

Practice Tips

  • Start with stationary balls
  • Practice from different angles
  • Gradually increase distance
  • Focus on technique over power
  • Record and analyze your shots
