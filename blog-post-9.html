Power Shot Techniques

Master the power shot - a fundamental technique for generating maximum force and accuracy in your shots.

Key Techniques

  • Proper run-up approach
  • Plant foot positioning
  • Ball contact point
  • Full follow-through
  • Body momentum transfer

Common Mistakes

  • Poor run-up timing
  • Incorrect plant foot placement
  • Weak follow-through
  • Leaning back during shot
  • Inconsistent contact point

Body Positioning Tips

  • Maintain forward momentum
  • Keep head down during contact
  • Use arms for balance
  • Transfer weight through the shot
  • Stay over the ball

Practice Tips

  • Start with stationary balls
  • Focus on technique before power
  • Practice from different distances
  • Work on both feet
  • Build up power gradually
