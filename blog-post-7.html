Dribbling with Cones Techniques

Master the art of dribbling through cones to improve your ball control and agility. Perfect for home practice.

Key Techniques

  • Close ball control
  • Quick direction changes
  • Using both feet
  • Maintaining proper speed
  • Looking up while dribbling

Common Mistakes

  • Looking down at the ball
  • Taking too large touches
  • Not using both feet
  • Moving too fast for control
  • Poor body positioning

Body Positioning Tips

  • Stay on your toes
  • Keep knees slightly bent
  • Maintain low center of gravity
  • Use arms for balance
  • Keep body between ball and cones

Safety Tips

  • Always warm up properly before training
  • Start with simple patterns
  • Build up complexity gradually
  • Take rest days between intense sessions
  • Stay hydrated and maintain proper nutrition
