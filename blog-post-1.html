Dribbling & Control Techniques

Mastering dribbling and ball control is essential for any soccer player. Here are the key techniques and common mistakes to be aware of.

Key Techniques

  • Keep your head up to maintain awareness of surroundings
  • Use both feet for better control and unpredictability
  • Maintain close ball control with small touches
  • Use body positioning to shield the ball from defenders
  • Keep your body between the ball and defender

Common Mistakes

  • Looking down at the ball too much
  • Poor body positioning
  • Not using both feet equally
  • Lack of spatial awareness
  • Rigid body movement

Body Positioning Tips

  • Stay on your toes
  • Keep knees slightly bent
  • Maintain low center of gravity
  • Stay balanced and ready to move
  • Use arms for balance

Safety Tips

  • Always warm up properly before training
  • Focus on proper technique before speed
  • Build up intensity gradually
  • Take rest days between intense sessions
  • Stay hydrated and maintain proper nutrition
