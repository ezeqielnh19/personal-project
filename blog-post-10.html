Crossing and Floated Pass Techniques

Master the art of crossing and floated passes - essential skills for creating goal-scoring opportunities.

Key Techniques

  • Proper approach angle
  • Ball contact point
  • Follow-through direction
  • Body positioning
  • Pass trajectory control

Common Mistakes

  • Poor approach angle
  • Incorrect ball contact
  • Insufficient follow-through
  • Wrong body position
  • Inconsistent trajectory

Body Positioning Tips

  • Open body to target
  • Plant non-kicking foot correctly
  • Maintain balance throughout
  • Use arms for stability
  • Follow through towards target

Practice Tips

  • Start with stationary balls
  • Practice from different angles
  • Work on both feet
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Gradually increase distance
